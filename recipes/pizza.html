Good and Pratical Pizza Recipe

a very pratical recipe to do a good pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup fat-free milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Step 2: Stir in milk and olive oil until a soft dough forms.
  3. Step 3: Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead 10 times.
  4. Step 4: Shape dough into a ball.
  5. Step 5: Cover dough with an inverted bowl and let sit for 10 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Roll dough into a 12-inch circle on a baking sheet.
